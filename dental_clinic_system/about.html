Founder & Chief Dentist

Dr. Vijay B. Wagh

B.D.S. (Mumbai) | M.D.S. (Prosthodontics)

Dr. Vijay B. Wagh is the visionary founder and principal dental surgeon at Swami Dental Care Centre. With over 18 years of dedicated practice in advanced dentistry, he has transformed the dental healthcare landscape in our community.

His expertise spans across complex dental implantology, full-mouth rehabilitation, cosmetic dentistry, and laser dentistry. Dr. Wagh believes in providing personalized, pain-free dental care using the latest technological advancements.

He regularly attends international dental conferences and workshops to stay updated with global best practices, ensuring that patients receive world-class treatment right here at Swami Dental Care.

Our Mission & Vision

At Swami Dental Care Centre, we are driven by a singular mission: to provide exceptional, comprehensive dental care in a compassionate and comfortable environment. We believe that everyone deserves a healthy, beautiful smile that boosts their confidence and quality of life.

Our vision is to be recognized as the most trusted dental care provider in the region, known for our clinical excellence, innovative treatments, and patient-centered approach. We strive to create a dental experience that exceeds expectations at every visit.

We are committed to continuous learning and improvement, ensuring that we always offer the most advanced and effective dental solutions available in modern dentistry.
